FAQ
Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What financing options are available?
We partner with premium lenders to offer competitive rates starting at 3.9% APR. Our finance team can structure leases, traditional loans, or balloon payments tailored to your needs. We also accept cryptocurrency and wire transfers for immediate purchases.
Do you offer nationwide delivery?
Absolutely. We provide enclosed transport delivery anywhere in the continental United States, included complimentary for vehicles over $100,000. International shipping is available to select countries with full customs handling.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day/500-mile return guarantee on all certified vehicles. If you're not completely satisfied, return the vehicle in the same condition for a full refund minus a $500 restocking fee. No questions asked.
Can I schedule a test drive?
Test drives can be scheduled online or by calling our concierge team. We offer both on-site experiences at our Beverly Hills showroom and complimentary at-home test drives within a 50-mile radius for qualified buyers.
How does the trade-in process work?
Simply fill out our online trade-in form or bring your vehicle to our showroom. Our certified appraisers will provide a market-competitive offer within 30 minutes. We consistently offer above KBB values for well-maintained vehicles.
Are your vehicles certified pre-owned?
Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a comprehensive 200-point inspection by factory-trained technicians. We provide a complete CARFAX history report, remaining manufacturer warranty details, and our own 12-month/12,000-mile supplemental warranty.
Do you source specific vehicles on request?
Yes, our acquisition team specializes in locating rare and specific vehicles worldwide. Whether you're seeking a particular color combination, limited edition, or classic model, we leverage our global dealer network to find exactly what you want.
What about after-sale support?
Every APEX client receives a dedicated relationship manager, priority scheduling at our service center, complimentary annual detailing, and invitations to exclusive automotive events. Our support never expires — once an APEX client, always an APEX client.
