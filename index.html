MindCare – Mental Health SaaS Platform

Problem

New users struggled to understand what MindCare offered and dropped off during onboarding, leading to low activation and under-utilized therapist tools.

Approach

Mapped end-to-end flows for patients and therapists, clarified value propositions on key screens, and simplified choices in onboarding to reduce cognitive load.

Introduced guided steps, contextual microcopy, and a more accessible visual hierarchy that works in low-light environments.

Outcome

The updated flows improved clarity, reduced unnecessary branching, and made it easier for teams to iterate on new features without rewriting the UX.

Meesho Redesign – Marketplace Experience

Problem

Shoppers faced inconsistent product cards, cluttered detail pages, and unclear checkout steps, hurting trust and increasing drop-offs.

Approach

Reworked the navigation and information hierarchy, introduced consistent card patterns, and clarified primary actions for each step of the journey.

Prototyped key flows and validated them with user scenarios focused on discovery, comparison, and repeat purchases.

Outcome

The redesign created a more consistent and scalable UI system, making it easier to launch campaigns without rethinking layout each time.
